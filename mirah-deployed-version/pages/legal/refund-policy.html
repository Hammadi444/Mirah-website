1. Overview

This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which Mirah will issue refunds for subscription fees, transaction charges, and other services. We are committed to customer satisfaction while maintaining fair business practices.

By subscribing to our Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to this Refund Policy. This policy should be read in conjunction with our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.

2. General Refund Policy

IMPORTANT: All subscription fees are generally non-refundable. By subscribing to Mirah, you agree that refunds will only be issued under specific circumstances outlined in this policy.

This policy applies to:

  • Monthly and annual subscription fees
  • One-time setup fees and onboarding charges
  • Transaction fees and per-order charges
  • Premium feature add-ons and upgrades
  • Overage charges for exceeding plan limits

3. Situations Where Refunds Are Granted

3.1 Technical Service Failures

We will issue partial or full refunds if the Service experiences prolonged outages or technical failures that prevent you from using core functionality:

  • Complete Service Outage: If the platform is completely inaccessible for more than 24 consecutive hours, you may be eligible for a pro-rated refund
  • Critical Feature Failure: If Shopify sync, courier booking, or order management fails for more than 48 hours due to our technical issues
  • Data Loss: If we lose your data due to our technical failures (not covered by backups), we will issue a full refund for the affected month
  • Security Breach: If a security breach on our end compromises your account data

EXCLUSIONS: Refunds will NOT be granted for outages caused by third-party services (Shopify API, courier APIs, payment gateways), internet connectivity issues, or force majeure events.

3.2 Billing Errors

If you are incorrectly charged due to our billing system error:

  • Duplicate charges for the same subscription period
  • Charges after successful cancellation (if cancelled before renewal date)
  • Incorrect pricing applied (different from published rates on our website)
  • Unauthorized charges without your consent

You must report billing errors within 30 days of the charge for refund eligibility.

3.3 Service Misrepresentation

If we have materially misrepresented the features or capabilities of our Service on our website or marketing materials, and you can demonstrate that:

  • The Service does not provide core features explicitly promised during sign-up
  • We failed to disclose major limitations that affect your ability to use the Service
  • The Service is fundamentally different from what was advertised

Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of discovering the misrepresentation, with clear evidence of the discrepancy.

3.4 Early Cancellation of Annual Plans

For annual subscriptions, we offer limited refund options:

  • Within First 30 Days: If you cancel within 30 days of signing up for an annual plan, we will refund 80% of your payment (20% processing fee retained)
  • After 30 Days: No refunds for cancellations after the first 30 days, but you can continue using the Service until the end of your annual term
  • Unused Months: We do not issue pro-rated refunds for unused months in annual subscriptions

3.5 Regulatory or Legal Compliance Issues

If Pakistani regulatory authorities (PTA, FBR, SECP) mandate that we discontinue services to your business or region, we will issue a pro-rated refund for the unused portion of your subscription.

4. Situations Where Refunds Are NOT Granted

4.1 Change of Mind

We do not issue refunds simply because you:

  • No longer need the Service
  • Found an alternative platform you prefer
  • Decided to close your business or stop selling online
  • Underestimated the learning curve or setup time required
  • Did not use the Service after subscribing

4.2 Monthly Subscriptions

  • Monthly subscription fees are never refundable, even if you cancel mid-month
  • You can continue using the Service until the end of your current billing period
  • No pro-rated refunds for partial month usage

4.3 Free Trial Conversions

If you:

  • Were charged after a free trial ended (you did not cancel before trial expiration)
  • Forgot to cancel your trial and were automatically billed

No refunds will be issued as you provided payment information upfront and agreed to automatic billing upon trial completion.

4.4 Third-Party Charges

Mirah is not responsible for and will not refund:

  • Courier Fees: Shipping costs, COD charges, fuel surcharges, return fees charged by TCS, Leopards, M&P, etc.
  • Shopify Fees: Shopify subscription costs, transaction fees, or app charges
  • Payment Gateway Fees: Stripe, PayPal, JazzCash, or Easypaisa processing fees
  • SMS Charges: Bulk SMS costs for customer notifications

These are separate services billed directly by third parties. Any refund requests must be directed to the respective service provider.

4.5 Account Violations

No refunds will be issued if your account is terminated due to:

  • Violation of our Terms and Conditions
  • Fraudulent activity or misuse of the platform
  • Engaging in prohibited activities (fake orders, illegal products, money laundering)
  • Abusive behavior toward our staff or other users
  • Security threats or hacking attempts

4.6 Technical Issues Beyond Our Control

No refunds for service disruptions caused by:

  • Shopify API outages or changes to their API policies
  • Courier API failures or courier service disruptions
  • Internet service provider (ISP) issues on your end
  • Device compatibility or browser-related problems
  • Force majeure events (natural disasters, power grid failures, government shutdowns)

4.7 Feature Limitations

No refunds if you:

  • Misunderstood the scope of features included in your plan
  • Expected features that were never promised or advertised
  • Exceeded plan limits (order volume, API calls, users) and were charged overage fees
  • Required custom development or integrations not included in standard plans

5. Refund Request Process

5.1 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you must:

  • Step 1: Contact our support team via email at support@mirah.com or through the in-app support chat
  • Step 2: Provide your account email, subscription details, and transaction ID
  • Step 3: Clearly explain the reason for your refund request with supporting evidence (screenshots, error logs, etc.)
  • Step 4: Wait for our team to review your request within 5-7 business days

5.2 Refund Review Process

Our team will:

  • Verify your subscription and payment history
  • Investigate the technical issues or billing errors claimed
  • Review logs, system performance data, and support tickets
  • Assess eligibility based on this Refund Policy
  • Communicate the decision via email within 7 business days

5.3 Refund Processing Time

If approved:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Refunds will be processed within 10-14 business days
  • Bank Transfer: Refunds may take up to 7-10 business days
  • Mobile Wallets: JazzCash and Easypaisa refunds process within 3-5 business days
  • International Payments: Stripe or PayPal refunds may take up to 10 business days depending on your bank

IMPORTANT: Processing fees (typically 2-3% of transaction value) charged by payment gateways are non-refundable and will be deducted from your refund amount.

5.4 Refund Denial

If your refund request is denied:

  • We will provide a detailed explanation via email
  • You may appeal the decision within 14 days with additional evidence
  • Final decisions are made at Mirah's sole discretion
  • You may escalate unresolved disputes through legal channels as outlined in our Terms and Conditions

6. Pro-Rated Refunds

In eligible cases, pro-rated refunds are calculated as:

  • Formula: (Subscription Fee ÷ Days in Billing Period) × Unused Days
  • Example: For a PKR 10,000 monthly subscription cancelled after 15 days: (10,000 ÷ 30) × 15 = PKR 5,000 refund

NOTE: Pro-rated refunds are only issued in cases of prolonged service failures or regulatory compliance issues, not for voluntary cancellations.

7. Chargebacks and Disputed Charges

7.1 Contact Us First

Before initiating a chargeback with your bank or payment provider, please contact us directly. Most billing issues can be resolved quickly through our support team.

7.2 Chargeback Consequences

If you file a chargeback without contacting us first:

  • Your account will be immediately suspended pending investigation
  • Access to the platform and all data will be disabled
  • If the chargeback is found to be unjustified, your account may be permanently terminated
  • We may report fraudulent chargebacks to credit bureaus and payment processors
  • You will be responsible for chargeback fees (typically PKR 2,000-5,000) imposed by payment processors

7.3 Legitimate Chargebacks

We understand chargebacks may be necessary for:

  • Unauthorized transactions (if someone else used your payment information)
  • Billing after clear evidence of cancellation was ignored
  • Duplicate charges not resolved through our support team

In these cases, please provide documentation and we will cooperate with your bank's investigation.

8. Cancellation Policy

8.1 How to Cancel Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Log in to your Mirah dashboard
  • Navigate to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription
  • Confirm cancellation and provide optional feedback

8.2 Cancellation Effects

  • Monthly Plans: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You can continue using the Service until then.
  • Annual Plans: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your annual term. No pro-rated refunds for unused months.
  • Access: After cancellation, your account will be downgraded to read-only mode for 30 days, allowing data export.
  • Data Retention: Your data will be permanently deleted 90 days after cancellation unless required by law to retain longer.

8.3 Reactivation

If you wish to reactivate your account after cancellation:

  • You may reactivate within 90 days by contacting support
  • You will be charged for a new billing period
  • Previous subscription discounts may not apply
  • After 90 days, all data is deleted and cannot be recovered

9. Downgrade Policy

If you downgrade from a higher-tier plan to a lower-tier plan:

  • Monthly Plans: Downgrade takes effect immediately. No refund for the current month. You will be charged the lower rate on your next billing date.
  • Annual Plans: Downgrade takes effect at the end of your annual term. No pro-rated refunds for unused portion of higher-tier plan.
  • Feature Access: Premium features will be immediately disabled upon downgrade.
  • Data Loss: If your new plan has lower limits (e.g., order volume, users), excess data may be archived or deleted.

10. Upgrade Policy

If you upgrade to a higher-tier plan:

  • Monthly Plans: You will be charged the pro-rated difference immediately and the full new rate on your next billing date.
  • Annual Plans: You will be charged the pro-rated difference for the remaining term of your current annual subscription.
  • Feature Access: Premium features are activated immediately upon upgrade.

11. Special Circumstances

11.1 Business Closure or Bankruptcy

If your business closes or declares bankruptcy:

  • You must notify us immediately and cancel your subscription
  • No refunds will be issued for unused time
  • Outstanding fees remain due and payable
  • You may request data export before account closure

11.2 Death of Account Holder

In the unfortunate event of the account holder's death, a family member or legal representative may contact us with a death certificate to:

  • Cancel the subscription immediately
  • Request a pro-rated refund for unused time (at our discretion)
  • Obtain data export for business continuity

11.3 Natural Disasters or Force Majeure

If a natural disaster, pandemic, or other force majeure event prevents you from operating your business, we may offer:

  • Temporary account suspension without cancellation
  • Payment plan extensions or deferrals (case-by-case basis)
  • Pro-rated refunds at our discretion

Contact our support team to discuss accommodation options.

Changes to this policy do not apply retroactively to charges made before the update. By continuing to use the Service after policy changes, you accept the updated terms.
