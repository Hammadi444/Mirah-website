FAQ

Pricing questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before starting your free trial.

Is there a free trial?
Yes every plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You can explore the workflow, test courier booking, and see tracking + profit flow before paying.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. You can cancel anytime. Your subscription won’t renew after cancellation. Your data remains safe and accessible based on your account access rules.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a fair refund policy depending on usage and timeline. If you face a genuine issue, contact support we’ll review and help responsibly.
What happens after the trial ends?
You can upgrade to Starter, Growth, or Pro. If you don’t upgrade, your account switches to limited access until you choose a plan.
Can I get a custom plan for my operation?
Yes. If you have high volume, multiple stores, or custom workflow needs, contact sales for a tailored plan.
